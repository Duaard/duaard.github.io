LLM Limitations #

Part of the initial research was to understand some of the limitations of current LLMs especially in respect to my project. The most concerning is probably between *Understanding Context *****and the Lack of Intuition/Emotion.

LLMs are good at pattern recognition but may struggle with complex contexts or nuanced scenarios. This means that two similar situations with slight variations may lead to different interpretations or actions for a human that can identify such nuances.

The lack of human intuition or emotional understanding kind of falls in the same umbrella. The trained model answers without variations depending on mood like a human would. This can perhaps be alleviated by including emotions by being explicitly verbose on the current state and including it in the training data.
